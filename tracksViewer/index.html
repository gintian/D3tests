Tool to explore tracks withs stops:
Shows stops and distance travelled in different ways -- as map, as time and distance equally scaled, as time scaled dependent of distance and as distance scaled dependent of time. Currently the following tracks can be viewed: Currently the following tracks can be viewed:
  • Napoleon's march on Moscow (Winter 1812)
  • Single GPS track of walk
  • Two orienteering runs
  • Multiple bus-trips on a Dublin bus-line:
    • All 3 trips together
    • Bus 15088
    • Bus 15061
    • Bus 15025

Part of work done for the paper:
M.-J. Kraak, B. Köbben, and Y. Tong.
Integrated time and distance line cartogram: a schematic approach to understand the narrative of movements.
Cartographic Perspectives, (77):7–17, 2014. doi: 10.14714/CP77.1234.
